[h2]Auto Reforge Plus[/h2]
Original mod: [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2707400823]Auto Reforge[/url]
Optimized version of Auto Reforge mod, one-click reforge equipment to the specified prefix, freeing your hands.
Optimization: This mod allows you to freely drag the reforge window to adjust its position, and automatically memorize the window position. In addition, you can also set the X and Y coordinates of the window in the settings interface.
